Staged Depth-Pruning Distillation of a Flow-Matching Text-to-Speech Teacher: A Compact Hindi Speech Synthesizer ⭐

📅 2026-07-21👥 Sivateja Alukur Trikutam et al.
A practical recipe for building a compact Hindi TTS model by distilling a 337M-parameter flow-matching teacher (IndicF5) under a ~17.6-hour data budget. Using staged depth pruning from 22→16→12→8→6 transformer blocks with WER-gated fine-tuning, the students reach WER 0.00 at 249M and 190M params. The 131M model remains robust; at 102M a capacity cliff is observed. The method yields a high-quality Hindi voice running in real-time on a 6GB laptop GPU.

A Unified Embedding Space for Text, Image, Video, and Audio ⭐

📅 2026-07-21👥 Abdul Basit Tonmoy et al. (EximiusLabs)
Fusion Embedding family adds audio to a frozen vision-language embedding base. Gen 1 trains only a 16.4M connector between frozen audio tower and frozen base; Gen 2 adds modality-gated deep adapters (44.2M).
